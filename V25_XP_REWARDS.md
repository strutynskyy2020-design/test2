# TM6 Bonus v25 — XP rewards

- Daily tasks: easy +10 XP, medium +20 XP, hard +30 XP after admin approval.
- Weekly goals: +100 XP together with +200 Point.
- Monthly bonus goal: +300 XP together with +1000 Point.
- XP is added to `total_xp`, shown in task/goal UI, and recorded in transaction metadata.
