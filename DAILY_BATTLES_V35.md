# Daily Battles V35

- Every Kyiv calendar day approved employees are paired deterministically in a shuffled order.
- An odd employee is paired with TM6 Bot (0 points).
- Battle score is the sum of positive point transactions earned during that Kyiv day, excluding battle rewards.
- On the first request after midnight, unfinished battles from prior days are settled.
- The winner receives +50 Point to balance, total earned and leaderboard transactions.
- A tie gives no reward.
- The Tasks page shows the current opponent and live score, refreshing every minute and on app focus.
- Leaderboard supports the `day` period using exact Kyiv-day boundaries.
